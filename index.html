A Day for Everyone

Caister Festival is a free family community event celebrating the brilliant village of Caister-on-Sea. Come and enjoy live music, fantastic food, local stalls, and a wonderful atmosphere — all in the heart of the village.

🎵

Live Music

Local and regional acts performing all day on the main stage.

🍔

Food & Drink

Street food stalls, local vendors, a beer tent, and refreshments.

🎡

Fairground

Rides and activities to keep the little ones entertained all day.

🛍️

Local Stalls

Browse a wide range of local craft, community, and trade stalls.
